Melamina
2018
Typeface & Booklet

Melamina is a typeface inspired melamine tablewear. Melamine is an organic compound with various applicationsm. In the post war period, it was commonly used as tablewear, and has recently made a trendy resurgence. The type is a sans-serif rounded type, useful smaller portions of text that needs a bit of fun.
