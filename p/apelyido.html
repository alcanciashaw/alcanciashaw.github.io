Apelyido
2017
Website & Typeface

Two adaptations of Penelope Flores’ essay “How Filipinos Got Their Surnames”. This essay explains why many Filipinos have Spanish surnames today. Dictated by Spanish colonialists, the indigenous populations of the Philippines were renamed by approved Spanish names with no choice. This project is an exploration of this essay in both and web and print format — I aimed to see how the same information can be either set in an interactive or tangible setting.
